Menu Details

Free Verification Count

You can verify 1,000 free users with OTPless. You can see the remaining credits on the upper left side in the Billing section page.

Balance Information

OTPless is a prepaid system. You can top up your OTPless balance with your credit card and use the credits in your account. You can view your account balance on the upper left side in the Billing section page.

What Information Is on the Dashboard?

After the OTPless integration into your app is complete and you start verifying phone numbers, you can view the number of successful verifications and incomplete varification users. You can filter your result by the app names and dates.
