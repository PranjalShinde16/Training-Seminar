Topping up the Account

OTPless is a prepaid system. If you’d like to use the Professional Plan, you’ll need to add funds to your account.

How Do I Add Funds to My Account?

After logging in to your OTPless account, click Billing from the menu.

You can choose suitable option from screen to buy new credits.

How Do I Know When My Balance Is Running Low?

When your OTPless account balance drops below 100, we’ll send you a notification e-mail to the address associated with your account.

What Happens if My Balance Runs Out?

After your balance reaches zero, your account remains active for 7 days and you can continue to use OTPless as normal during this grace period. If no funds are added within 7 days, the applications associated with OTPless will become inactive and you won’t be able to use OTPless until you add funds.
