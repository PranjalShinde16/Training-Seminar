What is OTPless?

OTPless is a verification solution which allows your users to sign up for your app seamlessly, without the need for a phone number and one-time password for verification. Besides being reliable and customizable, it lets your users choose their preferred platform.

Is OTPless available in my country?

OTPless is a global solution. Verifications using WhatsApp is available throughout the world.

Is this verified by WhatsApp?

Yes. We have official integration with WhatsApp and we use verified WhatsApp Business APIs.

Do I need to setup an account or integrate with WhatsApp?

No, you don't. We take care of everything. You just need to consume our APIs.

Is there any additional charges to be paid to WhatsApp??

Absolutely not. We have a very transparent pricing structure that has no hidden charges, not even tax or payment gateway charges.

Which account will be shown to the users of my app during the verification process?

The OTPless account will be displayed to your users by default.

Can I use OTPless only for mobile apps?

You can use OTPless for both mobile apps and web-based apps.

How often can I view the number of verifications performed with OTPless?

You can instantaneously track the number of verifications via the dashboard display.

How can I track my verification expenses?

Aside from the number of verifications, you can track your verification costs instantaneously on the dashboard display.
